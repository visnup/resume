#  [Visnu Pitiyanuvath][github]

### [Node.js Knockout]
##### Co-creator, organizer (2010-present)

Node.js Knockout is a 48-hour hackathon featuring [node.js]. It's an online,
virtual competition with contestants worldwide. 2012 is the third year it's
been held. [People love it][nko love].

### [Fortnight Labs]
##### Partner (2010-present)

Consulting for startups. We help early-stage startups go from ideas to actual
released products (web or mobile) very quickly. We're comfortable researching
and defining a minimal product all the way to building it, using whatever
technology fits.

### Swivel
##### Engineer -> CEO (2006-2010)

Swivel was an early big-data startup that made it easy to share data and
charts. Ruby on Rails, mysql backend; JavaScript charting.

#### Guru -> Unicru
##### Engineer (2000-2005)

At Guru.com, worked on a resume & job matching engine in Java. After being

techniques (WEKA).

#### [Duke University]
##### B.S. Electrical Eng. & Computer Science (1996-2000)
